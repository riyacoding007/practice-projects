Latte

A latte is a smooth coffee drink made with one part espresso and three parts steamed milk, topped with a light layer of milk foam. Perfectly balanced and creamy.

Macchiato

An espresso “stained” with just a dash of milk. Bold and strong, with a hint of creaminess.

Black Coffee

Pure, unfiltered strength — black coffee is brewed without milk or sugar, offering the raw and authentic flavor of roasted coffee beans.

Espresso

Espresso is a concentrated coffee brewed under high pressure. With a rich crema and deep aroma, it forms the base of many coffee drinks.

Mocha

A delicious fusion of espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate. Sweet, comforting, and perfect for those who love coffee with a dessert-like twist.
